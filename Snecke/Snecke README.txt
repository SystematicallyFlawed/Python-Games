In-between Ping and the 'game' that is Bugged, there was Snecke.
Snecke is a simple game of cat and mouse between an ever growing black snake of pixels and a green pixel. If the snake hits the wall or itself, it dies.

#Controls#
W - Move north
A - Move west
S - Move south
D - Move east
